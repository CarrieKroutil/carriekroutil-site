I led the **Content & Curation team at Degreed** as Engineering Manager. This is a pointer to a
couple of the features we built — the kind of work that lives behind a login, so rather than
recreate it here I'm linking to the write-ups my designer, [Khalid](https://khalidl.nl/), put
together from a design perspective.

## Pathways

[**Pathways**](https://khalidl.nl/degreed-pathways) let people curate a learning *experience* —
not just a pile of links, but an ordered, guided path through content. The goal was to make
learning feel like a journey with a clear next step, and that focus paid off in higher
**completion rates** for the learners who followed them.

## Live Events

[**Live Events**](https://khalidl.nl/degreed-live-events) turned out to be unexpectedly timely.
When COVID shut everything down and the world moved virtual overnight, Live Events gave people a
**streamlined place to discover and join virtual events** — exactly the moment that capability
mattered most.

For the full design story behind each — the problems, the decisions, the screens — head to
Khalid's write-ups linked above.
