AI is reshaping how software gets built, and managers need a working grasp of it — not just the theory, but the tools in their own hands. This section runs the full arc: the AI literacy every EM needs, what AI-assisted and AI-native engineering actually change in practice, and hands-on on-ramps to the tools themselves — Claude Code and BMAD, plus using them as a second brain for the leadership job, not only for code. It closes on the hardest part, which is human: guiding a team through adoption without mandates, fear, or a quiet loss of rigor.
