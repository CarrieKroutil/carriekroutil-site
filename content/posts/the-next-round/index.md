People assume golf is about the number at the end. For me it's the opposite: it's the
standing invitation to go play again. A rough hole just means there's a next one, and a
good one means let's run it back.

That's the whole appeal — there's always a next round, and I'm always up for it. Bring
your clubs, bring a dog, bring nothing but a good attitude. I'll see you on the first tee.
