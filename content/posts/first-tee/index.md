If you're looking for swing tips, equipment reviews, or a breakdown of my latest score, you might be disappointed.

I play golf, but I rarely remember what I shot.

What I do remember are the mountains, the sunshine, the sound of a well-struck ball, and four hours where the noise of everyday life finally gets quiet.

Golf isn't my competition.

It's my therapy.

This blog is for anyone who loves the game—not because it's perfect, but because it gives us a reason to slow down, be present, and enjoy the walk.

There will be stories, lessons, adventures, and maybe a few bad shots along the way.

Grab your clubs.

Let's play.
