I built this site greenfield with the BMAD method

June 22, 2026 · 6 min read
hero image (optional)

I wanted a little corner of the internet that was actually mine — somewhere to post about golf, my dogs, code, and whatever else I'm into. Here's the surprising part: going from an empty folder to a live site took an afternoon, not a weekend.

Where I started

No design, no plan, just an idea and a domain. I used the BMAD method to turn the idea into a brief, then a look-and-feel, then the actual build.

photo: my desk + a dog photobomb

Publishing is just markdown

The thing I care about most: posting has to be effortless. It is.

# write a new post
hugo new content content/posts/my-golf-weekend.md
# ...write markdown, drop in photos...
git push   # Amplify builds & it's live in ~2 min
No CMS, no dashboard, no friction. Write, push, done.

That's the whole trick. If you've been meaning to make your own site, this is your sign.
