## What shipped

- **4.1** Styled tag term pages at `/tags/{topic}/` — all posts under a topic, newest first; light "check back" empty state.
- **4.2** Header full-text search — **satisfied by the existing Hextra FlexSearch config** wired back in Story 1.3; verified titles + body indexed, focusable aria-labelled control, graceful no-JS degradation.

## What went well

- **The smallest epic, because earlier work paid off.** Search (4.2) needed no new build — the FlexSearch config from Story 1.3 already covered it. The win was *recognizing* that and verifying it (index coverage, focus, no-JS fallback) rather than rebuilding.
- **Term pages reused the post-card partial** again — consistent listing UI with no new markup, fourth epic running.
- **The one accepted JS-dependent feature degrades gracefully** — FR-9's explicit contract was honored.

## What was hard

- Genuinely little. This epic was mostly verification and styling on top of foundations already laid. That's the dividend of the scaffold-and-pin discipline from Epic 1 and the shared-partial discipline from Epic 2.

## Lessons

1. **Check what you already have before building.** Story 4.2 could have been a from-scratch search implementation; instead it was a verification pass on config that already existed. Always audit prior work against a new story's ACs first.
2. **Small epics are a signal of good earlier architecture**, not under-scoping.
